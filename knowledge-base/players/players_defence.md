# Defensive Players

## Centre Backs
Centre backs are responsible for defending central areas.
They stop attackers, win aerial duels, and organize the defensive line.

Key traits:
- Strength and aerial ability
- Tackling and clearances
- Defensive positioning
- Communication

## Full Backs
Full backs operate on the sides of defense.
Modern full backs contribute both defensively and offensively.

Important attributes:
- Stamina
- Crossing ability
- Defensive awareness
- Recovery pace

## Defensive Philosophy
Defenders focus on structure, compactness, and minimizing space.
They are essential in maintaining team shape and preventing goals.
