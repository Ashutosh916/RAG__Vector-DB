# Midfield Players

## Central Midfielders
Central midfielders link defense and attack.
They control tempo and distribute the ball across the pitch.

Important qualities:
- Passing range
- Vision
- Positional awareness
- Ball retention

## Defensive Midfielders
Defensive midfielders protect the backline and break up opposition attacks.
They often sit in front of defenders.

Key attributes:
- Tackling
- Interceptions
- Positioning
- Physical strength

## Playmaking Role
Midfielders dictate the rhythm of the game.
They are involved in build-up play, ball progression, and defensive transitions.
