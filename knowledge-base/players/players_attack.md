# Attacking Players

## Strikers
Strikers are responsible for scoring goals and occupying central attacking positions.
They often operate inside the penalty box and rely on movement, finishing, and positioning.

Key attributes of strikers:
- Finishing
- Off-the-ball movement
- Composure
- Heading ability

## Wingers
Wingers play on the flanks and provide width to the attack.
They often dribble past defenders and create chances through crosses or cut-inside runs.

Common winger traits:
- Pace and acceleration
- Dribbling skills
- Crossing accuracy
- 1v1 ability

## Attacking Playstyle
Attacking players thrive in high-tempo systems and pressing teams.
They benefit from quick transitions, through balls, and overloads in wide areas.
