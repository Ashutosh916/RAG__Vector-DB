# Goalkeepers

## Shot Stoppers
Goalkeepers are the last line of defense.
Their primary role is to prevent goals through saves and positioning.

Key skills:
- Reflexes
- Shot stopping
- Positioning
- Handling

## Sweeper Keepers
Modern goalkeepers act as an extra defender.
They participate in build-up play and sweep behind the defensive line.

Important attributes:
- Ball playing ability
- Decision-making
- Communication
- Awareness outside the box

## Goalkeeper Responsibilities
Goalkeepers organize the defense and control the penalty area.
They play a crucial role during set pieces and defensive transitions.
