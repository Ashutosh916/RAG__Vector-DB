# Football Statistics Explained

## Key Metrics

### Expected Goals (xG)
- Measures quality of chances created
- Based on shot location and context
- Helps evaluate performance beyond scoreline

### Possession Percentage
- Indicates control of the ball
- High possession does not always mean dominance

### Pass Completion Rate
- Measures accuracy of passing
- Important for midfield control

## Defensive Metrics

### Tackles Won
- Number of successful tackles
- Shows defensive activity

### Interceptions
- Reading the game and cutting passing lanes
- Key for defensive midfielders
