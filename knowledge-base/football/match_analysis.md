# Football Match Analysis

## Pre-Match Analysis
- Study opponent formation
- Identify pressing triggers
- Analyze defensive weaknesses

## In-Game Observations
- Shape without the ball
- Distance between lines
- Effectiveness of substitutions

## Post-Match Review
- Compare xG vs goals
- Evaluate individual performances
- Identify tactical adjustments for next match

## Common Match Scenarios
- Breaking down a low block
- Defending a one-goal lead
- Chasing the game late
