# Football Tactics Overview

## Common Formations

### 4-3-3
- Emphasizes width and pressing
- Commonly used by possession-based teams
- Requires technically strong wingers and fullbacks

### 4-2-3-1
- Balanced defensive and attacking structure
- Double pivot helps in buildup and defensive cover
- Popular in modern transitional football

### 3-5-2
- Uses wing-backs for width
- Strong central overload
- Effective against teams that press high

## Tactical Concepts

### Gegenpressing
- Immediate pressure after losing possession
- Aims to recover the ball quickly
- Requires high fitness and coordination

### Low Block
- Team defends deep near their penalty area
- Reduces space behind defenders
- Effective against stronger opponents
