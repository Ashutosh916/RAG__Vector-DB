# Football Transfer Strategy

## Budget-Based Recruitment

### Low Budget Clubs
- Focus on youth and free transfers
- Look for undervalued players
- Prioritize resale value

### High Budget Clubs
- Buy proven performers
- Target tactical fits rather than star names
- Depth is as important as quality

## Scouting Factors
- Age and injury history
- Tactical compatibility
- Mental attributes like work rate and decision-making

## Data-Driven Recruitment
- Use metrics like xG, progressive passes
- Compare league-adjusted statistics
- Combine data with scouting reports
