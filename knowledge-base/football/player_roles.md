# Football Player Roles

## Defensive Roles

### Ball-Playing Defender (BPD)
- Comfortable on the ball
- Initiates build-up from the back
- Important in possession-based teams

### Defensive Midfielder (Anchor Man)
- Shields the defense
- Breaks opposition attacks
- Maintains positional discipline

## Attacking Roles

### Inverted Winger
- Starts wide but cuts inside
- Often strong foot opposite to flank
- Creates shooting and passing lanes

### False 9
- Drops deep instead of staying central
- Confuses center-backs
- Creates space for wingers to exploit
